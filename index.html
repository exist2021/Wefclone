First Principles: Life & Earth

If Life is a product, Energy is the MVP. Earth and Life are One Input/Output Machine. Life breathing in oxygenated air (and breathing out carbonated air) is synchronous with Earth breathing in carbonated air (and breathing out oxygenated air). There is practically no time difference between the two production/consumption cycles for any economic arbitrage to be created by a retailer or a banker. Earth and Life are not two separate entities, with zero room for middlemen.

Earth Economics & Life Sovereignty

Sovereignty is the principle that Life is its own God. Applied to the human species, Sovereignty is the principle that every human is her own government. This principle is why 3.2 billion humans will vote to elect their representatives across 65 countries in 2024 alone. From here, it is but a small step for humanity to align our planet's economics to the principle of Sovereignty. We are championing a "One Earth, One Humanity" Economy that makes us all co-owners of Earth.
